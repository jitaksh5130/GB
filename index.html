About Green Bharat

We serve farmers with high-quality organic and chemical solutions, focusing on yield, safety and long-term soil health.

Who We Are

We manufacture a large array of high-quality organics and chemical pesticides, fungicides, herbicides and plant growth regulators that aid cultivators in attaining optimum farm yields. Our in-house R&D continually develops specialized formulations and validates them through government-approved laboratories in India and abroad.

We are committed to maintaining industrial standards, worker safety and pollution-free production activity while ensuring that our products are environmentally safe and secure.

Vision & Mission

  • Serve farmers with the best inputs and enhance farm yields.
  • Provide safe, effective organic and chemical crop protection solutions.
  • Continuously innovate agrochemical formulations.
  • Deliver high levels of customer satisfaction and farmer smiles.
